# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-16

Atlas is a community project. It is designed to avoid advertising, tracking, resale of personal data, and unnecessary collection.

## What We Collect

If you create an account, we may store:

- Your username and public profile fields.
- Your email address or provider identifier through the authentication provider.
- Stores you mark as visited, including optional visit dates and notes.
- Friend relationships and pending friend requests.
- Store corrections, new store suggestions, moderation comments, and photo uploads you submit.
- Basic technical logs needed to keep the service secure and reliable.

If you use location features, location access is requested by the mobile operating system. The app should use location locally to show nearby stores unless a feature clearly asks to save or submit it.

## What Is Public

Public profile fields, public visit lists, approved photos, and accepted data contributions may be visible to other users if you choose to publish them.

Private visits and private notes should remain private to your account.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not run ads.
- We do not use third-party behavioral tracking SDKs.
- We do not publish your email address.
- We do not publish your precise live location.

## Moderation

When you submit a correction or photo, maintainers may receive a Discord notification containing the submission summary, your username, and reviewer links. Photos remain pending until approved.

## Data Deletion

Users should be able to request deletion of their account data, visits, photos, and pending submissions. Accepted factual contributions may remain in the public dataset, but personal attribution can be removed where practical.

## Contact

Open an issue in the project repository for privacy questions or deletion requests until a dedicated contact address is configured.
