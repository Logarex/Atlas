# Code of Conduct

Atlas should be useful, welcoming, and careful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but verify facts.
- Credit sources and contributors.
- Avoid speculation in the canonical dataset.
- Keep moderation decisions transparent and reversible where possible.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Uploading copyrighted photos or copied proprietary data.
- Publishing private information.
- Deliberately submitting false data.
- Evading moderation decisions.

Maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, restrict accounts, or block participation when needed to protect the project.
